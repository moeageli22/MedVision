# MedVision website build

MedVision

Empowering Future Medics Through Guidance, Mentorship, and Support

MedVision is a non-profit educational platform that helps aspiring medical students enter medical school. It provides resources, mentorship, and community support to guide students through every stage of their journey, including UCAT preparation, interviews, and personal statements.

Mission

To make medical education accessible, inclusive, and supportive for everyone, regardless of background.

Key Features

Mentorship programs that connect students with current medical professionals.

UCAT and interview preparation materials with expert guidance.

Community groups for sharing experiences and advice.

AI-powered chatbot for instant answers and study support.

Modern, responsive web platform designed for accessibility and simplicity.

Tech Stack

Frontend: Next.js / React

Hosting: Vercel

Design: Tailwind CSS, Figma

Email Integration: Resend / EmailJS

Domain: medvisionn.vercel.app

About the Project

Developed by Mohammed Ageli as part of a client collaboration to build MedVision’s official online platform. The website aims to provide a user-friendly, professional experience that supports the organisation’s mission and connects its growing community of aspiring medical students.
